Your Shifts, Simplified

Track shifts, manage schedules, and stay on top of your time — all in one place.

Whether you're juggling multiple jobs or just want a clearer picture of your work week, Shift Calendar makes it easy to keep everything organized.

View Your Hours

See a detailed weekly and monthly breakdown of your total hours worked and profits in multiple jobs profiles at a time.

Easy Management

Easily and quickly make shift change, work hours change or paid vacation requests to your manager and check their approval status.

Export Information

Archive and download a summary of your shifts as a PDF file. Perfect for filing taxes, or keeping a personal record of your work history.
